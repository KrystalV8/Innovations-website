The way biometrics works is by analyzing and identifying individual and unique attributes of someone. Biometrics uses computer science algorithms such as binary numbers.

They use binary numbers as an algorithm to identify the features of what is given to the computer. The computer then uses those numbers to analyze and match the features to an individual.
This is great for society as the need for privacy grows, and since every individual has their very own unique features, its considerably has to impersonate someone or simply swipe their credit card. However, that doesn’t mean it’s impossible. If you’ve ever seen a movie that has to do with national security, FBI, or even some kind of top secret intelligence, chances are, that you’ve seen biometric scans happen. Even in the Disney Pixar movies, The Incredibles 1 & 2.
